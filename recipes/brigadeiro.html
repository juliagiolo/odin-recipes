Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a popular Brazilian dessert. It's a sweet, creamy chocolate treat. Often described as a truffle or bonbon. This dessert is a staple at birthday parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Combine condensed milk, cocoa, and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until thickened, about 10 minutes.
  2. Transfer mixture into a shallow bowl and allow to cool.
  3. Pinch off some of the dough and roll into a small ball; place on a plate. Repeat with remaining mixture, then roll each brigadeiro in chocolate sprinkles.
  4. Enjoy or chill brigadeiros until ready to serve.
